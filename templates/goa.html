Goa

Goa is one of India's most popular tourist destinations, known for its vibrant beaches and nightlife.

Baga Beach is famous for its shacks, parasailing, and energetic crowd. The coastline stretches beautifully with golden sand and turquoise water.

Another gem of Goa is the iconic Dudhsagar Waterfalls.

The falls are a must-visit during monsoon, surrounded by lush forests and misty air.
